1. False peer disconnect / auto reconnect
Sometimes the app shows “peer disconnected” even when neither user intentionally disconnected.

- Do not treat temporary ICE state changes like “disconnected” as a final hang-up.
- Add auto reconnect logic when connectionState or iceConnectionState becomes:
  - disconnected
  - failed
- Attempt to reconnect without forcing users to re-enter the room.

  - “Connection interrupted… reconnecting”
  - “Reconnected”

Do not show “peer disconnected” immediately just because ICE temporarily changes to disconnected.
